Who is
Sneha Enterprises?

Sneha Enterprises is a Bihar-based registered agri-export company specializing in sourcing and exporting India's finest agricultural commodities — grains, spices, fox nuts (makhana) and fresh vegetables — to international buyers worldwide.

With 2+ years of focused export experience, we operate directly from the heart of Bihar — giving us unmatched access to premium makhana, paddy, and grain-growing regions of North and East India.
