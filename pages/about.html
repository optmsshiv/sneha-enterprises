About Sneha Enterprises

16 Years of Trusted
Global Agri Trade

Founded in 2008, Sneha Enterprises began as a domestic grain trader in the heart of Bihar and has since grown into a respected international exporter serving buyers across Asia, Africa, the Middle East and Europe.

Our strength lies in deep farmer relationships, rigorous quality control, and a team that understands both Indian agriculture and global market requirements. We build long-term trade partnerships.
