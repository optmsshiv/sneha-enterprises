Who We Are

Sneha Enterprises is an India-based export and import company headquartered in Patna, Bihar — the heartland of India's finest agricultural produce. We specialize in sourcing, processing and exporting premium agri-commodities including grains, spices, fox nuts (makhana) and fresh vegetables to buyers across the globe.

With 2 years of focused export experience and deep roots in Bihar's farming communities, we have built a trusted supply chain — connecting verified Indian farmers directly to international buyers across the Middle East, Southeast Asia, Africa, and Europe.

We are a registered exporter from India — holding IEC Code, GST Registration, FSSAI License and APEDA certification. Every shipment is fully documented, lab-tested and compliant with international import standards.
