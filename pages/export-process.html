FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity? ▼

MOQ varies by product — typically 1 MT for fox nuts, 5 MT for spices and vegetables, and 10–25 MT for grains. We can discuss smaller trial orders for first-time buyers.

How long does shipment take? ▼

From order confirmation to port loading typically takes 7–15 days depending on the product and quantity. Sea transit varies by destination: UAE (10–12 days), SE Asia (15–20 days), Europe (25–30 days).

Do you provide product samples? ▼

Yes, we dispatch samples worldwide before bulk orders. Sample cost and courier charges are borne by the buyer and later adjusted against the first order payment.

What quality certifications do you have? ▼

We hold IEC Code, GST Registration, FSSAI License, APEDA Registration and Phytosanitary certification. Lab test reports for moisture, purity and pesticide residue are provided with every shipment.

What payment terms do you accept? ▼

We accept TT (Telegraphic Transfer), Letter of Credit (Sight/Usance), DP and DA. For new buyers, we typically work on 30–50% advance + balance before shipment or against documents.

Can you arrange door-to-door delivery? ▼

Yes, we can arrange CIF (Cost Insurance Freight) or DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) shipments where we handle shipping, insurance and in some cases customs clearance at the destination.
